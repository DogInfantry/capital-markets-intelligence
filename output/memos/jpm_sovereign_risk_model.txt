VI. POLICY IMPLICATIONS & RECOMMENDATIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. EM Currency Crisis is the highest-risk tail scenario. Countries with
     large external financing needs (Turkey, South Africa, Brazil) are
     most exposed. Monitor FX reserves and current account balances.

  2. Fed hawkishness transmits globally through the USD channel.
     DM sovereigns face manageable yield increases; EM sovereigns face
     amplified capital outflow risk.

  3. Oil shock creates winner/loser divergence: Saudi Arabia and US
     benefit; India, Japan, and Turkey face stagflation pressure.

  4. Global recession scenario shows classic flight-to-quality:
     US, Germany, and Japan yields fall while EM spreads widen.
