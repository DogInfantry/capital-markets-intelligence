IV. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Energy sector consolidation continues to drive mega-deal activity.
     Expect further Permian Basin and shale asset consolidation in 2025.

  2. Technology M&A faces heightened regulatory scrutiny. AI/cybersecurity
     deals draw particular attention from DOJ and international regulators.

  3. PE-backed take-privates remain a viable exit path, particularly in
     fintech and healthcare where public market valuations are compressed.

  4. Cross-border deals require extended timeline assumptions. CFIUS review
     and national security considerations are no longer edge cases.

  5. Healthcare/pharma M&A is driven by pipeline acquisition rather than
     operational synergies — focus due diligence on clinical risk.
